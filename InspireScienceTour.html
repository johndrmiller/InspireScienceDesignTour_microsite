It’s science made simple—for you and your students. Let’s start with the easy navigation…

The sliding lesson menu makes it easy to get to the day’s lesson!

The slide-out menu has everything listed for easy locating—and it slides closed to keep the page focused on the lesson.

Lessons are based on the familiar “5-E” model:

  • Engage
  • Explore
  • Explain
  • Elaborate
  • Evaluate

All the interactive resources are at point of use in your online planner. The resources your students see in the student center are also present here to give you a full view of how the different parts of the lesson will work together.

Helpful background and differentiated learning strategies are available for each lesson.

It’s easy to customize and organize your experience by starring your favorite resources.

For each of the 5 E’s, you will readily see the pacing, grouping recommendation and the "What to Use,” “What to Know,” and “What to Do" structure for seamless instruction.

Preview what standards are associated with the lesson, along with tips for instruction.

Follow the Science and Engineering callout for practices that align with NGSS.

Short on time? The Fast Track option shows you ways to do more teaching with less time.

Customizable lesson presentations with engaging imagery and a launch pad for digital videos, simulations, and interactives make it easy to prepare for each lesson.
